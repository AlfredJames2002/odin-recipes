How to make BBQ Ribs

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon
  • 1 tablespoon dry mustard
  • 4 pounds of baby back ribs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 1/2 cups of barbecue sauce

Directions:

Preheat oven to 250 degrees farenheit. Mix together the spices in a bowl. Remove the membrane from ribs. Evenly rub the spice mix into the meat. Line a baking pan with aluminium foil. Set a wire rack inside the pan. Wrap the ribs with aluminium foil and bake for 2 1/2 to 3 hours. Once that is done, unwrap ribs and set it on the wire rack. Brush the barbecue sauce on the ribs. Return to oven for another hour. Once that is done, your ribs are ready!
