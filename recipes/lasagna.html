How to make Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • ground beef
  • sausage
  • ricotta cheese
  • tomatoe sauce
  • garlic
  • oregano, basil, pepper mix
  • mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg
  • parsley
  • tomato paste
  • onion
  • lasagna pasta
  • parmesan cheese

Step 1: Cheese filling

Combine mozzarella, ricotta, 1 egg, and a couple table spoons of parsley into a bowl and mix.

Step 2: Meat Sauce

Combine Ground beef and sausage in a pan. Add the oregano, basil, and pepper mix and tomato paste into the pan. Dice onion and garlic into fine cubes and add to pan. Cook until brown. Add the tomato sauce and mix well.

Step 3: Lasagna

Boil the pasta in a pot of water until it cooks al dente.

Step 4: Layer and Bake

In a baking tray, add a layer of meat sauce. Then add a layer of lasagna pasta. Add a layer of cheese filling. Repeat until tray is filled. Bake on 300 degrees for 1 hour. Once it is done baking, add parmesan cheese and enjoy.
