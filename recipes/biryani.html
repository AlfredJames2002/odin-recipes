How to make Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken thighs (cubed)
  • 3/4 cup plain yoghurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of biryani masala
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1 1/2 salt
  • 2 cups of basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1/2 cup french fried onions
  • 1 tomato (grated)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/3 cup mint leaves chopped

Step 1: Chicken Curry

Mix the chicken thighs with the yoghurt, lemon juice, green chilli, ginger garlic paste, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and all of the listed spices. Marinate for an hour in the fridge. In a deep pot, add the ghee, fired onions, and tomato and cook until it forms a paste. Add the marinated chicken and cook for 15 minutes.

Step 2: Rice

Boil the basmati rice in a pot of water. Once it boils over, turn off the stove and drain the rice 10 minutes later.

Step 3: Combine

Add the cooked rice into the pot with the chicken Curry. Add the mint leaves and cilantro into the pot mix well. Your biryani is ready!
